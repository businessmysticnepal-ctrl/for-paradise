Our Mission

Empowering Lives, One Act of Kindness at a Time.

For Paradise was founded on the simple belief that everyone deserves a chance to thrive. We work tirelessly to address critical needs like hunger, lack of education, and homelessness within our community. Our approach is hands-on, compassionate, and focused on creating sustainable, long-term change.

Our vision is a community where every individual has the resources, support, and opportunity to reach their full potential.

What We Do

Food Security

We operate food banks and mobile soup kitchens to ensure no one in our community goes hungry.

Education Support

Providing scholarships, school supplies, and tutoring programs for underprivileged children.

Shelter & Housing

We offer temporary shelter for the homeless and assist families in finding stable, long-term housing.
